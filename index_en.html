About Me!

My name is Jorge Pinto. I am 29 years old and I am in the last year of IT Enginering Course in Lamego School of Technology and Management.

I began my professional career in energy and telecomunications area. During this path I did a Technical Course in Electricity and Automation (level 5) to acquire more skills and knowledge to get more opportunities and challenges in the world of work.

However, those did not fulfill me professionally. In fact, I feel that just being a superior technician It is not the only required condition for companies because It is essential to have access to a continuous formation to face new challanges. When I became financially stable and still working, I decided to try a new area that is evoluting and requires continuous training. As I am a keen on technology, I began my degree. I confess this course exceeded my expectations because this is helping me developing my skills in such a way that I would never think It could be possible, I try to learn more and more to consolidate my knowledge.

In my opinion, school/education does not prepare us for the labour market, but gives us the tools and knowledge to work with. Everyday I invest in my knowledge that is why I am attending foreign languages classes like English (I pretend to get C2 certificate). Last year, I started learning German because I realised the importance to comunicate in diferent languages as we are in a global world. I always try to be updated about new technology. For this reason I enjoy making expiriences at home with servers, VPN and NAS. I am aware of several webpages about tech development such as Codecademy where I have access to a lot of information about programming languages and IT in general, I make a lot of courses there.

Along my professional path, I was a team leader and responsible for some tasks. I like to work as a team, I am an organized and hard-working person, I try my best to achive my goals. I am also very creative because I am constantly thinking about new ideas and new projects.

My favorite hobby is making some electronic gadgets with microcontrolers such as ESP32, Arduino, Raspberry pie or pure eletronic. I like a lot the concept DIY (Doing your self) because I learn a lot with new peojects and there problems too.
